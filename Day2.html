Toastmasters International

Toastmasters International is a global nonprofit organization dedicated to improving communication, public speaking, and leadership skills. Founded in 1924 by Ralph C. Smedley, it has grown to over 16,000 clubs in more than 140 countries. The organization provides a structured and supportive environment for members to practice and enhance their speaking abilities. Members follow the Pathways program, which consists of projects designed to develop both speaking and leadership skills. Regular club meetings include prepared speeches, impromptu speaking sessions called Table Topics, and constructive evaluations. Toastmasters also hosts speech contests at local, national, and international levels to showcase talent and foster healthy competition. Participation helps individuals gain confidence, improve interpersonal skills, and advance in their professional careers.
